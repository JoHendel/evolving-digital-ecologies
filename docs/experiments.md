# Experiment Examples

## Mutation Sweep

File: `configs/experiment_mutation_sweep.yaml`

Research question: How do mutation rate and mutation strength affect adaptation, diversity, and extinction?

Primary metrics:

- Final population
- Extinction frequency
- Trait diversity
- Live lineages
- Mean metabolism and speed

## Environment Sweep

File: `configs/experiment_environment_sweep.yaml`

Research question: How do resource abundance and hazard damage change long-term stability?

Primary metrics:

- Population trajectory
- Resource efficiency
- Clustering index
- Collapse events

## Behavior Comparison

File: `configs/experiment_behavior_comparison.yaml`

Research question: Does neuroevolution produce more adaptive behavior than a transparent heuristic baseline?

Primary metrics:

- Final population
- Mean energy
- Extinction frequency
- Trait diversity
- Spatial aggregation
